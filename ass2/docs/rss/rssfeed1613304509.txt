He may have been out of the game - and out of games - for some time now, but his return feels like he never missed a beat, even when the journey gets a little bumpy.For those just getting acquainted, Tesla Effect: A Tex Murphy Adventure is a successfully Kickstarted entry in the Tex Murphy series of adventure games which began in 1989. The series came to be known partly for its use of full-motion video (FMV), where game designer Chris Jones took on the role of the titular gumshoe, Tex Murphy. Tesla Effect is the sixth entry in the series, and once again features FMV performances for its cutscenes.Yes, a game in 2014 is using FMV cutscenes. It's retro and a bit dorky, but it's also fun to see real people surrounded by CG environments that are not at all convincing.
